Navigation Guide

Navigate to each page of voting india follow these easy steps

1. This is your personalized Dashboard where you can quickly preview all critical information related to the election process. It includes the following components: Your Vote Status Card,Top Candidates Card, Election Calendar,Countdown Timer, Past Candidate Records, Party Overview, Statistical Highlights

2. This is a candidates list where you can see who all the aspiring candidates for new election

3. Use this voting panel to review all candidates. Each one is shown in a separate card just click the 'Cast' button on your preferred candidate to vote.

4. This is the Voting Status page where you can monitor the current progress of the election. It includes various visual indicators such as:
(A) A detailed list of all current candidates along with their total votes.
(B) A live graph showing vote trends or comparisons between candidates.
(C) A pie chart that visually represents the vote distribution among candidates.
(D) Winning percentage to help identify who's currently leading.

Election Guidelines

  • 1. Eligibility Criteria: Only registered users are allowed to vote. Each voter must verify their identity (e.g., via email, ID, or token).
  • 2. One Vote Per Voter: Only one vote is allowed per voter for each election. Multiple attempts will not be counted or may be blocked.
  • 3. Vote Confidentiality: All votes are confidential and securely encrypted. No one can see who you voted for.
  • 4. How to Vote: Review candidate profiles. Click “Cast Vote” next to your selected candidate. Confirm your choice in the popup.
  • 5. Tampering & Misuse: Any attempt to tamper with the system may result in disqualification. Misuse or sharing of login credentials is prohibited.
  • 6. Support & Queries: For technical issues, contact: [Support Email or Number] FAQs and help are available in the Help section.
